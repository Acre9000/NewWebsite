Environment Concept: Gerda overpaints

In this project I made some marketing material for the special edition of "Gerda: A flame in winter" (Bird Island, 2022). I worked on top of early engine screenshots, having to introduce characters, different moods, details, etc. The end result was to be in tune with the game's painterly shaders.
