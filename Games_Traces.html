Games: Traces

Mobile game made with DADIU, featuring exploration on a semi open-world, and diverse player movement, using your shield to slide, surf and glide on a desert world with ancient ruins.

The entire team was 14 people, we worked on this game for around 4 months, and my role was Art Director. The main efforts were coordinating work from the 2 artists, and communication across teams, but also did some concept art, game logo, and some marketing images.
